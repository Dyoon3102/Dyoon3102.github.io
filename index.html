What are Gundam plastic models?

Also known as Gunpla, Gundam plastic models are plastic model kits depicting the giant mecha-robots from the long time running, very popular Japanese anime series Gundam. Gundam series started airing on Japanese televisions in 1980 and until today, there were a total of 38 series, each series consisting of tv series and movies. Each series demonstrated a different mobile suit (Gundam designs) that makes up the massive inventory on different types of Gunpla.

Different Gunpla Scales

  1. 1/35
  2. 1/60
  3. 1/72
  4. 1/100
  5. 1/144

Different Gunpla Grades

  • High grade (HG)
  • Master grade (MG)
  • Real grade (RG)
  • Perfect grade (PG)
  • Super deformed grade (SG)

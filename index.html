What are Gundam plastic models?

Also known as Gunpla, Gundam plastic models are plastic model kits depicting the giant mecha-robots from the long time running, very popular Japanese anime series Gundam.

Gundam Series

Gundam series started airing on Japanese televisions in 1980. Until today, there were a total of 38 series, each series consisting of many tv episodes and movies. Each series demonstrated a different mobile suit (Gundam designs) that makes up the massive inventory on different types of Gunpla.

Plastic Modeling

Plastic modeling is putting together plastic pieces that come in kits that are ready for assembly. Kits usually come in frames and requires you to pull out each piece. Some are assembled together using plastic glue and some are snapped together for final assembly. To give a desirable finish, painting is suggested.

Different Gunpla scales:

  1. 1/144
  2. 1/100
  3. 1/72
  4. 1/60
  5. 1/35

Different Gunpla grades:

  • High grade (HG)
  • Master grade (MG)
  • Real grade (RG)
  • Perfect grade (PG)
  • Super deformed grade (SG)
